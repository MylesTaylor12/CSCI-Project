My Favorite Videogame

One of my favorite videogames that I used to play back in high school was a game called Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. The game came out in 2009, but I did not end up acquiring it until 2010 for the Xbox 360. The videogame featured many different game modes, including Special Ops, Multiplayer mode, and Campaign mode.

Special Ops mode had different missions you had to complete with another player, and each mission had a specific task that you had to complete as you moved throughout whatever level you were playing. This game mode was very similar to Campaign mode; however, campaign mode had key differences. In campaign mode, each mission connected to the last, and all of the missions formed a storyline as a whole. Also, you were only allowed to play in single player mode in campaign mode. In Special Ops, you could play with two people using two different controllers.

Multiplayer mode allowed you to play with people globally using a broadband internet connection. My friends and I would play Multiplayer mode the most, forming our own teams and facing off against other people around the world. Great memories were formed.
